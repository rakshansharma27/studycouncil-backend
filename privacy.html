Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and educational role. We also store the queries you submit to the StudyCouncil platform to provide you with your history and to improve our caching systems.

2. How We Use Information

Your queries are sent securely to our partner APIs (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, Mistral) strictly for the purpose of generating educational responses. We do not use your personal data to train proprietary foundation models without explicit consent. Your email is used solely for account management and critical updates.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS. Your password is never stored in plain text.
