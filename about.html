About Us

Built for Students,
By People Who Were Students

StudyCouncil was born from a simple frustration: why trust one AI's answer when you can have a panel of the world's best models debate it for you?

Our Mission

Every student deserves access to the best possible explanation for every concept. We believe that's not achievable with a single AI model — no matter how good it is. Our mission is to make multi-AI learning accessible and affordable for every student, from Class 8 to PhD.

Why Multiple AIs?

Different AI models have different strengths. GPT-4 excels at structured explanations. Gemini is great with analogies. Claude is nuanced and careful. Mistral is fast and creative. When they work together — checking, critiquing, and building on each other — the result is dramatically better than any one of them alone. That's the StudyCouncil difference.

The Technology

StudyCouncil is inspired by the open-source llm-council project by Andrej Karpathy. We've built a student-focused product on top of this multi-LLM collaboration concept, adding study-specific modes, a clean interface, and features designed around academic needs — from concept understanding to exam preparation to career guidance.
